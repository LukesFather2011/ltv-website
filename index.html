Our Story

Built for producers,
by producers

Limitless TrueVibe started as a lofi YouTube channel, a cozy corner of the internet inspired by the lo-fi aesthetic, where producers could submit tracks and reach real listeners. It was a simple idea built on a genuine love for the music.

Word spread. People joined. And then the best question happened: "Do you only do lofi?" The answer was simple, no. We opened the doors to everything under the electronic music umbrella, and the community grew into something genuinely special.

Today, LTV is a free, community-run platform for producers to share original music, win monthly beat challenges, get featured on community playlists, and hang out with people who genuinely get it. No label fees. No gatekeeping. Just music.

  • Always free. No submission fees, ever
  • Open to all genres of electronic music
  • Community-designed merch for challenge winners
  • Run by an artist, for artists

Beat Challenges

Every month we drop a theme. You make a beat, the community votes, and the winner earns their "crown" — a custom shirt designed by the community itself.

How It Works

  1. 01

    Theme Drops in Discord

    Each month a new challenge theme is announced in the Discord server — a genre, a mood, a concept. Anything to spark creativity.

  2. 02

    Make Your Beat

    You've got the whole month. When you're ready, upload your .wav directly through the form. No links needed.

  3. 03

    Community Votes

    At the end of the month, the community listens and votes anonymously. Every member gets a say — community-run, not a committee.

  4. 04

    Win the Crown 👑

    The winner is crowned Beat Master in Discord and receives a limited-run shirt designed by the community.

🏆 The Beat Master Prize

Win and you'll earn the Beat Master title in Discord, get featured across LTV's channels, and receive a community-designed shirt. No entry fee, no purchase necessary. Just. Make. Music.
